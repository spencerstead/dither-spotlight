OBJ Dither Lens

Upload or drop a .obj file. The model is rendered once, then a lightweight Bayer post shader turns it into a realtime dither field. Hover the cursor lens to reveal the clean 3D model underneath.

Using built-in demo mesh. Drag an .OBJ anywhere to replace it.
